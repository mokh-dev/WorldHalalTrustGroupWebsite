Our Goal

World Halal Trust Group strives to provide the highest quality Halal Certification services and to assist its Clients by opening new markets and expanding trade horizons. Our main goal is to ensure the honest and qualified production of all Halal products. Halal Training Course

We seek to raise the awareness of our Clients and other companies about Halal industry. Every year we organise the Halal Training Course in order to provide an update on the latest Halal production and certification standards and trends and familiarize the companies with the main Halal requirements.

The yearly Course is compulsory for our Clients and is highly recommended for those companies, which consider becoming Halal Certified in the future.
